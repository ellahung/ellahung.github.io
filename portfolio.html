IMReCH - Website

I aM Review Class Helper

Duration: 10 month. This project was completed during the semester as part of my graduation project.
Deliverables: User research. Persona. Site map. Userflows. Sketches. UI.
Role: Leader. UI/UX Design. Assist Frontend Develop

Goals and Objectives

Our goal is to create a product that can streamline the review process by simplifying content access, enable more profitable organization through annotation features, and minimize time waste by providing efficient tools to manage recorded content.

Challenges

Similar software has limited recording functionality, and management difficulties lead to inefficiency and time waste for users when searching, organizing, and reviewing lecture content.
